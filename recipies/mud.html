Mud

Description

You may think that mud is just something you find lying around, but good quality mud (as any child knows) is not easy to manufacture.

The recipie here is for a fairly simple mud, but it should taste pretty good all the same.

Ingredients

  • Soil
  • Water

Steps

  1. Place the soil in a medium sized bowl
  2. Add a small amount of water, and stir.
  3. If the mud is a bit too thick, add more water.
  4. Enjoy
