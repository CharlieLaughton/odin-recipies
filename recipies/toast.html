Toast

Description

Toast is very lovely, but hard to make. It requires a lot of skill and patience if you want it to come out right - not something for beginners!

If you follow the instructions below to the letter, you should be OK though. Just don't hurry!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Using a sharp knife, cut two slices from your loaf
  2. Place them in your taoster - one slice in each slot
  3. Depress the lever to start the toaster
  4. When the cooked break pings up, remove it from the toaster
  5. Smear one side of each slice of toast with some butter
  6. Eat it
